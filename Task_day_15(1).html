About A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, the Missile Man of India, was born on October 15, 1931. He served as the 11th President of India.

Achievements

  • Developed SLV-III
  • Led nuclear tests in 1998
  • Wrote Wings of Fire
  • Bharat Ratna awardee
